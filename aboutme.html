About Me

There's not too much to tell about myself, I was born and raised in Maine, and now I'm back here for college. I took my first semester of school at Mississippi State University. This was a great experience to live somewhere other than the Northeast and see how the culture in different parts of America were.

I went to Mississippi State because I had the chance to try out for the football team, but unfortunately, the school was too far away for me and I decided to come back before my opportunity.

Hobbies

My favorite hobby is like a lot of typical Mainers, but I like to fish. In the Summer fishing takes up most of my free time. I mostly fish for bass, but I also like fishing for trout and would like to try ocean casting.

As another hobby, I like to travel different places in the world. So far I have been to a bunch of different places in Canada. I have also been to Hungary and Sweden in Europe also. My goal is to visit most of the countries in Europe.

Clubs/Organizations

My first semester of college at Mississippi State University I joined the club hockey team that was there. It was a very good experience and I met a lot of people through this.

At the University of Maine I have not joined any clubs yet, but I have become a pledge at Theta Chi Fraternity Gamma Chapter. This has been a great way for me to meet new people around campus and to get some good advice for my classes from the upperclassmen in the fraternity.
